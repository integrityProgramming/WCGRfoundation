The Windy City Guide Right Foundation was established in November 2020, by alumni members of the Theta Zeta Chapter of the Kappa Alpha Psi, Fraternity Incorporated who attended and graduated from Chicago State University and the University of Illinois, at Chicago. The purpose for the formation of this foundation is to provide financial assistance to inner city male youths who would not otherwise be able to pursue higher level academic opportunities. This foundation is organized exclusively for charitable, literary, scientific and educational purposes within the guidelines of the Internal Revenue Code.

By being a transformative force in the enablement for success to minority male youth from economically challenged and/or disadvantaged communities in and throughout the Chicago Metropolitan area.

To provided annual financial assistance in the form of scholarships by way of donors and fundraising efforts to individuals from economically challenged and/or disadvantaged communities who would not otherwise be afforded an opportunity to pursue a higher level of education and academia.
